EPITA

EPITA, the school of engineers in computer intelligence. Since its creation in 1984, EPITA is the first engineering school specializing in computer science in Paris. She prepares her passionate students in the engineering and digital professions to build the world of tomorrow. TEMPLATED

EPITA is ranked among the top computer engineering schools in France.

Founded in 1984, EPITA is a bilingual Graduate School of Computer Science. Thanks to its unique pedagogical approach, EPITA trains its students to become outstanding engineers who boast a solid base of scientific knowledge and the most advanced skills in the digital sector. EPITA also welcomes a wide range of foreign students thanks to its numerous international programs: bachelor, master of engineering, summer schools and French language courses.

International Bachelor of Computer Science

At EPITA, computer sciences are at the heart of our programs. We have designed a comprehensive Bachelor’s program of 6 semesters over a period of 3 years. At the end of the 3rd year, students have the possibility of pursuing their studies with a Master in English or in French.

Master of Science in Computer Science

The MSc is a certified degree that can be delivered only by a member of the CGE and accredited by the CTI (Commission des Titres d’Ingénieurs). The program enables students to acquire advanced skills and a high-level education, over a period of 18 months with a 6 months internship.

Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems

The MSc is a certified degree that can be delivered only by a member of the CGE and accredited by the CTI (Commission des Titres d’Ingénieurs). The program enables students to acquire advanced skills and a high-level education in the AI and Data Science fields, over a period of 18 months with a 6 months internship.

Master of Computer Engineering

Also certified by the CTI and delivered by the CGE, the ME is designed similarly to the MSc over a period of 18 months with a 6 months internship but. Students are particularly adaptable beyond their field of specialization and become renowned engineers.

French Language Centre

Located on the engineering school campus, the EPITA French Language Center has developed specialized know-how and a methodology adapted to teaching French as a foreign language to international students who wish to pursue their studies in France or more scientifically-oriented participants with specific goals.

Student Life and Services

EPITA encourages students to actively participate in one of its 40 clubs and associations, offering a unique opportunity to foster leadership development, complement the educational experience and build community.

EPITA is listed among the 15 best engineering schools

With four stars, the highest possible ranking, Challenges, the business magazine recognized EPITA as one of the six best post-baccalaureate engineering schools in France.

EPITA Research and Development Laboratory

Educating future engineers requires that professors be fully familiar with the most recent, cutting-edge technologies. In light of this, EPITA has three research laboratories and a digital startup accelerator.

Business Partnerships

EPITA aims at educating and training professionals who will be immediately operational in a company and able to develop technical expertise as well as acquire managerial and commercial skills.

Corporate relations

The corporate relations department has four main missions: assisting students in the development of their professional project, helping companies recruit new employees, maintaining relationships between graduates and the school, and encouraging partnerships.

Alumni

The EPITA Alumni Association brings together and offers assistance to all EPITA graduates who are active in the fields of innovation, new technologies, and digital development.
